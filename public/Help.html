What is a QR code?
A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of 2D barcode that stores information like a URL, text, or contact info. It can be scanned using a phone camera.
Can I save my QR code design?
Yes! If you're logged into your account, you can click the "Save" button and your current design will be saved to your account.
Do I need an account to generate QR codes?
No, anyone can generate a QR code without logging in. However, an account is required to save and manage QR designs.
What customization options are available?
You can customize the body color, background color, eye colors, gradient colors, body shape, eye frame, eye ball shape, and more.
Can I download my QR code?
Yes. After generating the QR code, you can right-click the image and select "Save As" to download it, or use the "Download" button if available.
How many QR codes can I save?
There is currently no hard limit. However, we recommend saving only the ones you need for easy management.
